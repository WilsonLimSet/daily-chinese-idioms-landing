**Pronunciation:** *lú huǒ chún qīng*  
**Literal meaning:** Furnace fire pure blue

## Origin & Usage

This idiom describes furnace (炉) fire (火) being pure (纯) blue (青). In traditional metallurgy, blue flames indicate the highest temperature and purest combustion. The phrase represents the pinnacle of skill development. Modern usage describes mastery that has reached the highest possible level, expertise refined to perfection.

## Examples

**English:** "Her technical skills have reached the highest level."

**Chinese:** 她的技术已经炉火纯青。
