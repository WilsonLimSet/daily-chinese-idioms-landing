**Pronunciation:** *guī gēn dào dǐ*
**Literal meaning:** Return root reach bottom

## Origin & Usage

Song Dynasty Neo-Confucian philosophers introduced this analytical method of returning to roots (归根) and reaching the bottom (到底). Ming Dynasty scholars expanded it from philosophy into practical problem-solving. The vertical metaphor of descending to foundations perfectly captured the search for fundamental causes. Modern analysis still seeks to distinguish root causes from surface symptoms.

## When to Use

**Situation:** Despite discussing various factors, cost remained the fundamental issue
