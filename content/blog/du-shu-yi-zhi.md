**Pronunciation:** *dú shù yī zhì*  
**Literal meaning:** Alone raise one banner

## Origin & Usage

This idiom describes alone (独) raising (树) one (一) banner (帜). It depicts establishing a unique position or style that stands apart from all others. The phrase values originality and distinction. Modern usage describes unique achievements, those who create distinctive approaches rather than following established paths.

## Examples

**English:** "His unique artistic style set him apart from all others."

**Chinese:** 他独特的艺术风格独树一帜。
