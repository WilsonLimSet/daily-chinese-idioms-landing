**Pronunciation:** *chǔ cái jìn yòng*
**Literal meaning:** Chu talent Jin use

## Origin & Usage

The strategic brilliance of the Jin state during the Spring and Autumn period gave us this phrase about utilizing Chu state's talent (楚材). Jin's systematic recruitment of capable individuals from rival Chu became a template for organizational growth through talent acquisition. Han Dynasty historians identified this strategy as key to Jin's rise to power. Modern corporations continue this ancient practice of strengthening themselves while weakening competitors through strategic hiring.

## When to Use

**Situation:** The rival corporation recruited top engineers from its struggling competitor
