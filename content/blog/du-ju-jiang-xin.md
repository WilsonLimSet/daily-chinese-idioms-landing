**Pronunciation:** *dú jù jiàng xīn*  
**Literal meaning:** Uniquely possess craftsman's heart

## Origin & Usage

This idiom describes uniquely (独) possessing (具) a craftsman's (匠) heart (心). It depicts original creative thinking with the skill of a master artisan. The phrase combines creativity and expertise. Modern usage describes innovative work that shows both originality and technical skill, design that demonstrates creative genius.

## Examples

**English:** "The architect's design showed remarkable ingenuity."

**Chinese:** 建筑师的设计独具匠心。
