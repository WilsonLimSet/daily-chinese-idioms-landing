**Pronunciation:** *chàng kōng chéng jì*
**Literal meaning:** Perform empty city strategy

## Origin & Usage

The 'Romance of the Three Kingdoms' immortalized Zhuge Liang's empty city strategy (空城计), where performing (唱) apparent confidence masked real vulnerability. Ming Dynasty writers transformed this specific historical incident into a broader strategic principle. The theatrical element proves crucial - success depends not just on the deception but on the convincing performance of calm assurance.
