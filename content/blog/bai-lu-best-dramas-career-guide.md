**Bai Lu (白鹿)** is one of the most consistently bankable actresses in Chinese drama — a ratings magnet whose premieres reliably trend and whose period and xianxia leads have repeatedly broken platform records. If you're discovering her through **The First Jasmine (莫离)**, her 2026 revenge-romance, here's the complete picture of how she got here and what's worth watching.

---

## Quick facts

- **Stage name:** Bai Lu (白鹿) · **Real name:** Bai Mengyan (白梦妍)
- **Born:** September 23, 1994, in Changzhou, Jiangsu Province
- **Agency:** Huanyu Entertainment (欢娱影视) — Yu Zheng's studio; signed in 2016
- **Breakout:** *The Legends* (招摇, 2019)
- **A-list year:** 2023 (three major leads in one year)
- **Best entry points:** *Till the End of the Moon* · *One and Only* · *Story of Kunning Palace*

---

## How she was discovered

Before acting, Bai Lu worked as an e-commerce model. In 2015 she appeared in short films from **Cattree Studio (猫的树)**, whose director recommended her to producer **Yu Zheng (于正)**; she signed with his Huanyu Entertainment in 2016 and began landing leads almost immediately. It's a classic Yu Zheng pipeline — take a fresh face and build a costume-drama star around her.

## The rise: from The Legends to A-list

Bai Lu's first lead came with **King Is Not Easy (2017)**, but her real breakout was **The Legends (招摇, 2019)** — her fearless demon-sect leader Lu Zhaoyao, opposite **Xu Kai**, made her a recognizable xianxia female lead. **Arsenal Military Academy** the same year (again with Xu Kai) confirmed her as a bankable young huadan (花旦).

The **2021 duology One and Only / Forever and Ever** (周生如故 / 一生一世, with Ren Jialun) — a tragic ancient love story continued as a modern reincarnation romance — became a fan phenomenon and won her major popularity honors.

Then came **2023, the year that sealed her A-list status**: three big leads in twelve months — the xianxia megahit **Till the End of the Moon**, the modern romance **Only For Love**, and the court-intrigue drama **Story of Kunning Palace**. *Till the End of the Moon* posted some of the year's strongest period-drama viewership and merchandise numbers, and *Story of Kunning Palace* was cited as a benchmark hit for a female-led drama.

## Best dramas to start with

1. **Till the End of the Moon (长月烬明, 2023)** — her biggest xianxia, opposite Luo Yunxi. The spectacle showcase.
2. **One and Only / Forever and Ever (2021)** — the tearjerker duology; start here if you want the emotional gut-punch.
3. **Story of Kunning Palace (宁安如梦, 2023)** — court scheming and reinvention; the most plot-driven.
4. **The Legends (招摇, 2019)** — where it began, with the Xu Kai chemistry that first made her a star.
5. **The First Jasmine (莫离, 2026)** — the new revenge-romance; the reason you're probably here.

## Why she's a ratings powerhouse

Bai Lu's strength is **audience pull**, not awards-circuit prestige — her trophies are overwhelmingly popularity and platform honors (Weibo Night, iQIYI Scream Night) rather than jury Best Actress prizes. But for a streaming-era lead, that pull is the whole game: record reservations, top viewership shares, strong merchandise sales, and a large international audience via WeTV, Viki, and YouTube licensed channels. It's exactly why **The First Jasmine** banked 4M+ reservations before airing.
