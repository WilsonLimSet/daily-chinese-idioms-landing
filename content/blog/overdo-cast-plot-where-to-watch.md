If you loved Zhang Linghe in **Pursuit of Jade**, his new drama is here: **Overdo (这一秒过火)**, a 1920s Republican-era Shanghai romance that started airing on July 19, 2026. Here's the cast, the premise, and where to watch it.

## Quick facts

- **English title:** Overdo
- **Chinese title:** 这一秒过火 (Zhè yī miǎo guò huǒ)
- **Episodes:** 33
- **Premiered:** July 19, 2026
- **Genre:** Republican-era (民国) romance / melodrama
- **Where to watch:** Viki, WeTV, and iQIYI (with English subtitles)

## The cast

- **Zhang Linghe (张凌赫)** as **Murong Qingyi** — the male lead. Zhang Linghe is fresh off leading **[Pursuit of Jade](/blog/pursuit-of-jade-history-behind-the-drama)**, one of 2026's biggest C-drama hits, so *Overdo* is the drama his fans have been waiting for.
- **Wang Churan (王楚然)** as **Ren Susu** — the female lead.
- **Fu Xinbo (付辛博)** in a key supporting role.

## The plot (no spoilers)

*Overdo* is set in the glamorous, dangerous world of **1920s Shanghai**. Murong Qingyi, secretly switched at birth, spends years enduring abuse from his adoptive father. Fleeing as a teenager, he's rescued by **Ren Susu** — but the rescue brings catastrophe down on her family.

Years later, the two cross paths again and slowly fall in love. Then, to protect the people around her, Ren Susu **fakes her own death** and disappears. Three years on, she returns — engaged to become Murong Qingyi's **sister-in-law**. It's a classic love-hate, fate-and-sacrifice melodrama built on the intense chemistry between the leads.
