**Pronunciation:** *yī fān fēng shùn*  
**Literal meaning:** One sail, wind favorable

## Origin & Usage

This idiom describes one (一) sail (帆) with favorable (顺) wind (风). It depicts a sailing voyage blessed with perfect winds requiring no struggle. The phrase is a common blessing wishing smooth progress. Modern usage wishes someone easy success, a life or endeavor free from obstacles, where everything goes smoothly.

## Examples

**English:** "May your journey through life be smooth and successful."

**Chinese:** 愿你的人生一帆风顺。
