## Role in Pursuit of Jade (2026)

**Character:** Xie Zheng (谢征, *Xiè Zhēng*), Marquis of Wu'an (武安侯), who initially conceals his identity under the alias **Yan Zheng** (言正).

**Plot role:** Xie Zheng is a young general nominally believed to be a frail, sickly scholar. In truth he is the noble Marquis Wu'an, gravely wounded after being betrayed and ambushed, harbouring a 16-year blood feud over his family's massacre. He survives by being rescued from a snowstorm by Fan Changyu (Tian Xiwei), a butcher's daughter in a remote village. To protect her family home, Fan Changyu proposes a "marriage of convenience" with the disguised Xie Zheng — he agrees, using her household as cover while plotting his revenge against the imperial court figures who destroyed his clan. Their contractual marriage gradually turns into genuine love as his identity is uncovered. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pursuit_of_Jade), [Baidu Baike entry for 谢征](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E8%B0%A2%E5%BE%81/65213153), [CPOP Home](https://www.cpophome.com/zhu-yu-zhang-linghe-tian-xiwei/))

**Relationship to other characters:**
- **Fan Changyu** (Tian Xiwei): wife of convenience → true romantic partner
- **Prime Minister Wei Yan** (Yan Yikuan): antagonist; tied to the blood-feud against Xie Zheng's family
- **Qi Min / Sui Yuanhuai** (Deng Kai): supporting figure intertwined with the political conspiracy
- The imperial court / 16-year feud is the central political throughline

**Source novel:** Yes, the character exists in the original web novel *《逐玉》(Zhu Yu)* by **Tuan Zi Lai Xi** (团子来袭). The Marquis-Wu'an / butcher-daughter "先婚后爱" (marriage first, love later) premise is from the novel. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pursuit_of_Jade), [Baidu Baike](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E9%80%90%E7%8E%89/64922142))

**Standout/fan-discussed moments:**
- The opening snowstorm rescue + forced cohabitation arc — heavily clipped on Weibo and YouTube
- The reveal scene where Fan Changyu learns Yan Zheng is actually Marquis Wu'an
- A semi-viral mocking nickname emerged on Weibo: **"粉底液将军"** ("Foundation-Liquid General") — netizens joked Zhang's general-character is so impeccably groomed in battle armour that his complexion stays flawless after bloodshed. Critics on Yicai and Zhihu argued the high-gloss styling pulled viewers out of the drama. ([Yicai](https://www.yicai.com/news/103114654.html), [Zhihu thread](https://www.zhihu.com/question/2019384773781537965))
- The fandom couple-name for the leads is **"Zhengyu"** (征玉) — coined from Xie Zheng + Changyu. ([Philstar](https://www.philstar.com/entertainment/2026/04/09/2519808/sepanx-real-zhang-linghe-tian-xiwei-dramas-watch-after-pursuit-jade))

## Breakout Role

**Two-stage breakout:**

1. **First breakout (recognition):** *Love Between Fairy and Devil* (苍兰诀, 2022) as **Chang Heng / God of War of Shuiyuntian**. He was the second male lead, not the lead — but his unrequited-love performance and visual charisma generated the dreaded "second-lead syndrome" wave on Weibo/Twitter/TikTok internationally. This drama, headlined by Esther Yu (Yu Shuxin) and Dylan Wang, streamed globally on Netflix/Viki/WeTV and is the moment most Western fans first noticed him. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Love_Between_Fairy_and_Devil), [Chasing Dramas review](https://www.chasingdramas.com/reviews/currently-watching-love-between-fairy-and-devil-%E8%8B%8D%E5%85%B0%E8%AF%80%EF%BC%89/))

2. **A-list confirmation:** *Pursuit of Jade* (逐玉, 2026) is the project that elevated him from "popular costume-drama actor" to **top-tier 顶流 (top-traffic) star**. The drama was the first mainland Chinese drama ever to chart on Netflix's weekly Global Top 10 Non-English TV list (#6 debut, climbed to #5; 1.9M views, 34.2M hours in week 1), retained Netflix Top-10 status for ~4 weeks accumulating ~1.6M views per week, drew 750M weekly all-platform views in China, totalled 3.3B+ aggregate views, and Zhang held the #1 character-popularity ranking (19,094 score) on Kuyun Data in week 11 of 2026. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pursuit_of_Jade), [MyDramaList News](https://news.mydramalist.com/article/chinese-drama-pursuit-of-jade-makes-its-netflix-top-10-debut), [Tonboriday](https://www.tonboriday.com/2026/04/pursuit-of-jade-smashes-33-billion.html), [Sixth Tone](https://www.sixthtone.com/news/1018355))

For SEO framing: most "zhang linghe" English searches in 2026 originate from one of two entry points — viewers who fell for Chang Heng in 2022 are returning, or new Pursuit-of-Jade Netflix viewers are searching him for the first time.
